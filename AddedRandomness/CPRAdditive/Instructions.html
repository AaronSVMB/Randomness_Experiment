This experiment is a study of group and individual behavior. Everyone in this experiment is receiving the exact same instructions. You will earn $7 simply for participating in this experiment for its full duration. But if you follow the instructions and make careful decisions, you can earn a significant amount of additional money. You will be paid at the end of the experiment via PayPal. The entire amount of money which you earned with your decisions throughout this experiment will be added up and paid to you at the end of the entire experiment.

During the experiment your earnings will be measured in points. Each point is worth X cents, so at the end of the experiment your points will be converted to dollars at a rate of XX points per dollar.

It is important that you remain silent and not look at other people's work during the experiment. If you talk, laugh, or exclaim out loud you will be asked to leave and will not be paid. If you have any questions or need assistance, raise your hand the experimenter will come to you.

Your Group

Each participant will be randomly assigned an ID number and randomly assigned to a four-person group. The group will remain the same throughout this portion of the experiment. There are 16 participants in today's experiment, so there will be 4 groups in all with 4 members in each. Because you are known only by your random ID number, there will be no way for anyone (including the experimenters) to know which group you are in or what decisions you make.

Your Decisions

This experiment consists of {{ C.NUM_ROUNDS }} decision-making periods. At the start of each period you will receive {{ C.ENDOWMENT }}. All other members of your group will also receive {{ C.ENDOWMENT}} each period.

In each period you will have to decide how to divide your points between two accounts.

Group's Joint Account: You are allowed to invest in your Group's Joint Account any whole number X between 0 and {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} (inclusive).

The payoffs you receive from your Group's Joint Account depends not only on the amount you invest in your Group's Joint Account, but also on the investment decisions of the other 3 members of your group. You will find the payoff formula for the Group's Joint Account below.

Personal Account: After investing in the Group's Joint Account, the remaining amount of your points will automatically be invested in your Personal Account.

For your Personal Account, the payoffs you receive depend only on your investment decision. Every point you invest in your Personal Account gives you a payoff of 1 point. If you invest X points in your Group's Joint Account, you will invest ( {{ C.ENDOWMENT}} - X) points in your Personal Account, and this will give you a payoff of ( {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} - X) points from your Personal Account.

Adjustment: After you and the other player's decisions are made, the computer will make adjustments to the earnings of all the participants in the experiment. The adjustments will be either +5 or -5 points. The same adjustment will be made to the earnings of the other members you are matched with, but the other groups in this session may receive different adjustments. The computer will choose the adjustment for your group based on a random number calculation, so that there will be a 50% chance that {{ C.additive_shock_value }} are added to the earnings of you and the other players you are paired with and a 50% chance that {{ C.additive_shock_value }} are subtracted the earnings of you and the other player you are matched with. The computer will calculate a different random number for all matched groups in this experiment.

Total payoffs: Your total payoffs per period are the sum of your payoffs from your Group's Joint Account +/- the adjustment to your Group's Joint Account, and your Personal Account.

Period by period information: You will not get information on the individual decisions of the other members of your group.
