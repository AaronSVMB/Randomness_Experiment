Welcome

This experiment is a study of economic decision-making. Everyone in this experiment is receiving the exact same instructions. You will earn $7 simply for participating in this experiment for its full duration. But if you follow the instructions and make careful decisions, you can earn a significant amount of additional money. You will be paid at the end of the experiment via PayPal. The entire amount of money which you earned with your decisions throughout this experiment will be added up and paid to you at the end of the entire experiment.

During the experiment your earnings will be measured in points. Each point is worth X cents, so at the end of the experiment your points will be converted to dollars at a rate of XX points per dollar.

It is important that you remain silent and not look at other people's work during the experiment. If you talk, laugh, or exclaim out loud you will be asked to leave and will not be paid. If you have any questions or need assistance, raise your hand the experimenter will come to you.

Your Matching

In this experiment you will be paired with one other player. You will be randomly paired with this player, and you will not know the identity of the participant you are matched with. Because you are known only by your random ID number, there will be no way for anyone (including the experimenters) to know who you or your partner are matched with or what decisions you make.

Your Decision

Both you and the other player will have two possible choices. You can choose Left or you can choose Right. If you both choose Left you will both get a payoff of {{ C.payoff_both_cooperate }}. If you both choose Right you will both get a payoff of {{ C.payoff_both_defect }}. If you choose Right but the other player chooses Left, you will get a payoff of {{ C.payoff_cooperate_defect_high }}, but the other player will receive {{ C.payoff_cooperate_defect_low }}. Likewise, if you choose Left but the other player chooses Right, then you will receive {{ C.payoff_cooperate_defect_low }} and the other player receives {{ C.payoff_cooperate_defect_high }}. After all players in the experiment have made their choices, the computer will report to you the move chosen by the other player and your payoff from this period of play.

You will play the one-period game just described to you for {{ C.NUM_ROUNDS }} periods with the same other player.

Adjustment

After you and the other player's decisions are made, the computer will make adjustments to the earnings of all the participants in the experiment. The adjustments will be either +5 or -5 points. The same adjustment will be made to the earnings of the other member you are matched with, but the other matched individuals in this session may receive different adjustments. The computer will choose the adjustment for your group based on a random number calculation, so that there will be a 50% chance that {{ C.additive_shock_value }} are added to the earnings of you and the other player you are paired with and a 50% chance that {{ C.additive_shock_value }} are subtracted the earnings of you and the other player you are matched with. The computer will calculate a different random number for all matched pairings in this experiment.

Example: If you both choose Left and the computer randomly picks to add {{ C.additive_shock_value }} your period payoff will be {{ C.payoff_both_cooperate }} + {{ C.additive_shock_value }} = 17.

Confidentiality

Your identity in the experiment will not be made known to any other participant at any time in the experiment. Your decisions and payoffs are confidential.

Do not discuss your choices or payoffs with any other player!

Thank you and Good Luck!
